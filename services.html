Sophrology & Relaxation Services

To know more about sophrology please see our FAQ

We will help you with: Stress and Anxiety; Sleep; Long COVID; Cancer; Depression; Burn-out; ADHD; Weight Management; Chronic health conditions for which stress and pain are major triggers; Perinatal and Parenthood Support; Life Transitions; Stressful Events.

All consultations are strictly confidential.

Please note that sophrology should NEVER replace medical advice and treatment.

Stress & Anxiety

Individual & Group Sessions

Over the course of 6 to 12 sessions, you will learn to address stress in your body, your emotions, and your mind. I will guide you with a combination of simple and effective techniques to practice and experiment with, to find your unique solution to redress your stress. By working in a modified state of consciousness we retrain your brain to feel less stressed, more positive and confident for a balanced you. I will coach you in setting up your regular personal practice, and give you advice on sleep, eating, and lifestyle that can complement your relaxation training, to feel more grounded and serene durably.

Sleep

Individual & Group Sessions

Sessions designed around sleep, blending relaxation with coaching; This includes looking into your sleep patterns, putting in place healthy sleep habits, and learning practical exercises to redress your stress level, and take care of racing thoughts, fears and anxiety, to feel more grounded and serene.

Cancer

Individual & Group Sessions

Provides techniques and tools aiming to deeply appease and help cope with cancer and its treatments. Using a variety of simple practical techniques, from mindfulness, gentle movements, and breathing to positive visualisations and self-hypnosis, we will aim to soothe body symptoms, redress stress, slow down thoughts, and regulate emotions by reconnecting you to your positive self.

  • 60 min sessions
  • in-person or online
